In a kingdom of old,
A thief once stole,
But caught he was,
And banished from the fold.
        
Years passed by,
And the thief became a name,
Notorious and sly,
But the princess remained the same.

Of the princess in danger,
He knew he had to be near,
To save her from a stranger.
        
He succeeded in his quest,
But was caught in the act,
The princess saw him,   
And her heart skipped a beat in fact.    
        
But the prince was gone,
And the kingdom was in turmoil,
The thief offered to help,
And the king agreed without a foil.
        
He proved his worth,
And won the hearts of all,
The thief had redeemed himself,
And the princess was enthralled.
        
Though she was married,
Her heart belonged to him,
And they lived happily,
Despite the odds and the grim. 
        
For sometimes, love finds a way,
Even in the darkest of days.
And redemption is possible,
