Learning to code is a challenging but rewarding journey that requires persistence and dedication. For some, coding may be a way to deal with rejection. By learning how to code, individuals can create something of their own, something that no one can take away from them. It's a way to express oneself and to build something that is truly unique. The process of coding involves trial and error, and sometimes things don't work out the way we want them to. However, with each failure comes a new opportunity to learn and improve. Through coding, individuals can develop a sense of resilience that can help them deal with rejection in other areas of their lives.
